This directory contains blog post images.

For optimal display, images should be:
- 16:9 aspect ratio (e.g., 1200x675px)
- Under 200KB for fast loading
- High quality with good contrast
- Relevant to the blog post topic

Image naming should match the slugs in the blog data:
- seo-ranking.jpg
- content-marketing.jpg
- local-seo.jpg
- algorithm-update.jpg
- seo-metrics.jpg
- backlinks.jpg
